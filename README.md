# Goofy Ahh Camera

Rice 442 is divided into two parts. One part is for office hours. The other part is used by the Computer and Network Security Club (aka CNS). Someone accidentally unlocked the doors to both parts of Rice 442, meaning that anybody can come into the CNS side and steal CNS's stuff (including tens of thousands of dollars' worth of computing equipment). As officers of CNS, this is a huge problem for us.

As a result, we've invented the Goofy Ahh Camera. It watches the door and scans for faces. If it detects a face that's not one of ours, it takes a picture and posts it for the world to see.
